## Quick Identification Key (Size & Shape)

### Seeds by Size Class

| Size Category | Length | Appearance | Common Examples | Habitat |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Tiny (under 1 mm)** | under 1 mm | Dust-like, round | Tobacco, petunia, poppy | Garden, ornamental |
| **Very small (1–3 mm)** | 1–3 mm | Round or ovoid | Lettuce, carrot, celery, thyme | Garden, herb, vegetable |
| **Small (3–6 mm)** | 3–6 mm | Ovoid, angular | Parsley, basil, mustard, sesame | Herb, spice, garden |
| **Medium (6–15 mm)** | 6–15 mm | Round, oval, bean-shaped | Beans, peas, lentils, sunflower | Legume, crop, wild |
| **Large (15–30 mm)** | 15–30 mm | Oblong, ovoid | Corn, acorn, walnut | Grain, nut, tree |
| **Very large (over 30 mm)** | 30+ mm | Elongated, thick | Coconut, avocado, mango pit | Tropical tree, fruit |

### Seeds by Shape Profile

| Shape | Features | Examples | ID Tips |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Round (spherical)** | Smooth, globe-like | Pea, lentil, coriander | Look for dimple or hilum (seed attachment point) |
| **Oval/Elliptical** | Even edges, flattened | Bean, sunflower, plum | Bilateral symmetry; check for distinctive patterns |
| **Angular/Faceted** | Multiple flat sides | Caraway, fennel, sesame | Count the ridges or corners |
| **Elongated (linear)** | 3–5× longer than wide | Carrot, parsnip, lettuce | Look for tapering ends |
| **Winged** | Thin membrane attachment | Maple, ash, elm, pine | Wings used for wind dispersal |
| **Flat (discoid)** | Very thin, disc-like | Pumpkin, melon, sunflower | Easy to identify by pressing |
| **Warty/Tuberculate** | Bumpy surface texture | Watermelon, cucumber | Distinct tactile feel |

## Seed Storage & Viability Reference

### Storage Conditions by Seed Type

| Type | Temperature | Humidity | Light | Duration | Container |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Dry seeds (grain, bean)** | 5–10°C | 10–15% humidity | Dark | 5–10 years | Sealed, paper/cloth |
| **Oil-rich seeds (sunflower, sesame)** | Refrigerate (2°C) | Low humidity | Dark | 2–3 years | Airtight glass |
| **Herb/spice seeds** | 5–15°C | Dry | Dark | 2–4 years | Sealed packet |
| **Large seeds (nut, tree)** | 1–5°C | Moist (not wet) | Dark | 6 months–2 years | Damp sand/peat in cold |
| **Short-lived (onion, parsley)** | 5°C | Dry | Dark | 1–2 years | Sealed envelope |

### Germination Rate Estimation

| Seed Age | Typical Viability | Test Method | Time |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Fresh/1 year** | 90–100% | Direct sow | 5–14 days |
| **2–3 years** | 70–90% | Cut test or soak | 8–21 days |
| **4–5 years** | 50–75% | Soak + warm | 14–30 days |
| **Over 5 years** | 25–50% | Extended soak + heat | 30+ days |
| **Very old (10+ years)** | 0–25% | Scarify + heat + time | Highly variable |

## Quick Reference: Identifying Crop vs. Weed Seeds

| Characteristic | Crop Seed | Weed Seed |
|---|---|---|
| **Size** | Usually larger (3+ mm) | Often tiny (under 2 mm) |
| **Uniformity** | Uniform within variety | Variable; many in clusters |
| **Surface** | Smooth or regular pattern | Often wrinkled or spined |
| **Smell** | Often pleasant (herb/spice) | Often musty or none |
| **Germination** | Fast, synchronized | Erratic; may lay dormant |
| **Viability** | Predicted by age | Unpredictable; very long-lived |
| **Density in soil** | Moderate (deliberate sowing) | Very high (wind/animal spread) |
