## Geographic Regions & Climate Zones

### Global Regions Reference Table

| Region | Latitude Range | Longitude Range | Climate Type | Primary Resources | Key Features |
|--------|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Equatorial Belt** | 10°N–10°S | Variable | Tropical rainforest | Timber, rubber, spices, cacao | High rainfall (2000+ mm/yr), dense vegetation, year-round warmth |
| **Tropical Savanna** | 10°–23° | Variable | Tropical dry/wet | Grass, game, hardwood | Seasonal rainfall, grasslands, scattered trees |
| **Subtropical Desert** | 23°–35° | Variable | Hot arid | Salt, minerals, sparse vegetation | Minimal rain, extreme heat, minimal water |
| **Temperate Forest** | 35°–60° | Variable | Temperate deciduous/coniferous | Timber, game, freshwater | Moderate rainfall (500–1500 mm/yr), four seasons, fertile soil |
| **Boreal Forest** | 60°–75° | Variable | Subarctic coniferous | Fur-bearing animals, timber, ice | Short growing season, permafrost, sparse vegetation |
| **Arctic/Tundra** | 75°–90° | Variable | Polar | Ice, migratory animals, minerals | Extreme cold, minimal vegetation, ice year-round |
| **Mountain Ranges** | Variable | Variable | Alpine/elevation-dependent | Minerals, timber (lower slopes), water sources | Steep terrain, thin air (5000+ m), alpine vegetation |
| **Coastal Regions** | ±5 km from shore | Variable | Variable (maritime moderation) | Fish, salt, shellfish, seaweed | Moderate temperatures, high-productivity zones, tidal zones |

## Map Interpretation Quick Reference

### Elevation Symbols (if using contour lines)

| Contour Interval | Elevation Gain | Terrain Type | Walking Difficulty |
|---|---|---|---|
| 10 m | 10 m per line | Flat/very gentle | Easy |
| 50 m | 50 m per line | Gentle slopes | Moderate |
| 100 m | 100 m per line | Moderate slopes | Difficult |
| 500 m | 500 m per line | Steep terrain/mountain | Very difficult |
| Tightly spaced lines | Rapid elevation | Cliffs/escarpments | Impassable on foot |

### Map Symbol Reference

| Symbol | Meaning | Navigation Impact |
|---|---|---|
| Blue (solid) | River/permanent water | Drinkable (check for minerals) |
| Blue (dashed) | Seasonal stream | Water only in wet season |
| Green/brown | Forest/vegetation | Slower travel, fuel available |
| Brown contours | Mountains/elevation | Altitude affects temperature & air |
| Pink/tan | Desert/sparse vegetation | No water, travel at night |
| Black line | Political boundary | Legal considerations, trade |
| Red circle | City/settlement | Resources, trade, shelter |
| Black dot | Village/small settlement | Limited resources |
